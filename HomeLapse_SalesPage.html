The Process

HOW IT WORKS

1
Generate your reference image
Use Midjourney, Leonardo AI or Gemini to create the initial room scene following the guide's character and environment details.
2
Paste Scene 1 prompt into Grok
Upload your reference image + paste the Scene 1 prompt. Grok generates a 10-second photorealistic clip.
3
Capture the last frame
Pause the video at the final second. Screenshot it. This becomes the reference image for Scene 2 — guaranteeing perfect continuity.
4
Repeat for all 9 scenes
Last frame + next prompt. Every scene continues exactly where the previous one ended. No inconsistency, no visual breaks.
5
Edit and publish everywhere
Join all 9 clips in sequence (~90 seconds total). Add background music. Post on YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels and Facebook.
